All About L'Amour Avec Passion Photography and Design

L'Amour Avec Passion is french for Love with Passion. The Owner Julie Juneau came up with the name because the business was a labour of love for her. She has been following her Passion in Photography and art since a young teenage girl. It is only when she attended college for the second time late in her years that she decided to make that passion her career. As student in Media Design many of her projects became works of art towards her business. She created the web page for Web page Design class. During her break she started taking jobs as a Wedding Photographer and the business began! She has many plans to grow and expand the business into a formal studio with focus on portraits, and life events. Please feel free to browse some of her work on the portfolio page and throughout the website.
