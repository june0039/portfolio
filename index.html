Welcome to L'Amour Avec Passion Photography

L'Amour Avec Passion has been a labour of love for Julie Juneau. She discovered her passion for photography as a young teen. It wasn't until she was in college that she re-discovered this passion and made it into a career of love, hence the name of the business. L'Amour Avec Passion french for Love with Passion. Her style is unique and her focus is on portraits, weddings, baby and life events, animal portraits and landscapes.
